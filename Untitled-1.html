beneath the weight of the early morning light,
everything is slow,
as if the world was caught in a deep breath waiting to be exhaled.

the sky, stretching out endlessly,
is not blue, nor gray,
but just a soft smear of color that fades in every direction,
dissolving into the horizon where the sea lies still.

the ocean sings,
not with sound,
but with the movement of its waves pushing and pulling,
its always present but each sound will never be the same.

The sand I stand on is cool,
damp from the night and the ocean,
each step leaving behind an indent in the ground,
an indent that will be soon washed away.

the wind, brushing against my skin like a memory that is almost lost,
but still lingers,
the heartbeat of the sea, the pulse of the wind, the quiet shuffle of the sand,
all blending into one continuous flow.

the clouds, barely there, thin, stretched out,
almost transparent as they drift across the wide expanse of the sky,
unhurried, unshaped, like thoughts passing.

time itself feels different,
slower, stretched out,
as if the world has forgotten the rush and business of the day,
suspended in the soft breath of the morning.

it is that in this place,
this moment,
that boundaries blur.

the line between the sky and the sea,
earth and air,
all dissolving into the soft edges of the morning.
